## body

Acting U.S. Attorney Duane A. Evans announced that**SAQUENA GRIFFIN,**a/k/a “QUEENIE,”****age 34, of New Orleans, pled guilty today toconspiracy to pay and receive illegal Medicare kickbacks which resulted in Medicare paying $378,274 to a local home health agency. According to court records,**GRIFFIN**was a recruiter and marketer for Comprehensive Nursing and Home Health Service, Inc. (Comprehensive) on Earhart Boulevard in New Orleans.**GRIFFIN**is charged with receiving illegal kickbacks to bring Medicare beneficiaries to Comprehensive. **GRIFFIN**faces a possible maximum sentence of five years imprisonment and a $250,000 fine. U.S. District Judge Jane Triche Milazzo set sentencing on October 19, 2017. Acting U.S. Attorney Evans praised the work of the Special Agents of the Federal Bureau of Investigation in investigating this matter. Assistant U.S. Attorney Patrice Harris Sullivan is in charge of the prosecution.
