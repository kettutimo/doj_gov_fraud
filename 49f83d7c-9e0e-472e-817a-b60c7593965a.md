## body

**NEW ORLEANS, LA –**U.S. Attorney Duane A. Evans announced that**CATHERINE CAMPBELL WILLIAMS**,****age 69, of New Orleans, Louisiana, pled guilty on May 31, 2022 to Theft of Government Funds, in violation of Title 18, United States Code, Section 641. According to court documents, beginning in December 2010 and continuing through 2020,**WILLIAMS**collected Social Security Administration benefits paid to a deceased relative. In total,**WILLIAMS**fraudulently received approximately $61,324 in Social Security Administration benefits to which she was not entitled. **WILLIAMS**faces a maximum penalty of ten (10) years imprisonment, followed by up to three (3) years of supervised release, a fine of up to $250,000.00, and a mandatory $100 special assessment fee. U.S. District Judge Greg G. Guidry set sentencing for August 30, 2022. U.S. Attorney Evans praised the work of the Social Security Administration, Office of the Inspector General. The prosecution of this case is being handled by Assistant U.S. Attorney Maria Carboni.
